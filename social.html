SOCIAL EVENTS

GATHERINGS

JMU GIVE hosts many parties throughout the year at the GIVE house downtown, Pouch. These are usually themed but also are often mixers. We also have a “big” event each semester, being ski trip or formal or semi-formal. Most parties are open, but bigger events are reserved for our most active volunteers as a thank-you!

Each semester, GIVE puts on a fundraiser for GIVE Headquarters’ Students 4 Students campaign. In the Fall, it is called Haydown and in the Spring it is called Leidown. We hire bands and buy lots of fun drinks and provide members with the opportunity to fundraise for their own international trips.

SMALL EVENTS

Creating a sense of community and stregnthening friendships is extremely important to our club. Because our club focuses on environmentalism, we often go on hikes, watch the sunrises/sunsets together, or take nice long weekend camping trips. Another popular gathering is our potlucks... givers know how to EAT!

This year, our wellness chair Kelly will be hosting “Wellness Wednesdays”. Primarily, these will be yoga sessions in UREC or on the lawn, but we are hoping to expand into more meditative and mental health boosting activities such as journaling.
