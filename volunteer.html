VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

Elementary Outreach

The Vine & Fig Educational Outreach Program seeks to educate and empower local youth around food, gardening, and nature. These volunteer opportunities include a range of activities such as local Elementary school trail clean-ups, creating & maintaining gardens, and facilitating educational activities for the local youth.

Jubilee Climate Farm

At the Jubilee Climate Farm, volunteers have the opportunity to participate in a number of exciting projects, all of which support environmental sustainability and growth, from planting trees and caring for local habitats to cultivating crop gardens and harvesting! If you go at the right time, you may even be fed a hojme-cooked meal...

Trail Maintenence

Through the Potomac Appalachian Trail Club (PATC), JMU GIVE has adopted the High Knob Fire Tower trail and a segment of the Shenandoah Mountain trail. The Potomac Appalachian Trail Club, through volunteer efforts, education and advocacy, acquires, maintains, and protects the trail and lands all up and won the east coast.

Visit Website

RHSPCA

The Rockingham-Harrisonburg SPCA works tirelessly to find homes for strays or surrendered animals in the local community. For this volunteer program, volunteers will have the opportunity to interact with a number of dogs at the SPCA by assisting in dog walking and recreation for this amazing organization! Who doesn't love cats and dogs?

Visit Website
Elkton Food Pantry

The Elkton Food Bank is an amazing opportunity to support those in need locally, while also forming relationships with the other, amazing volunteers from the community. This is a great way to get out of the JMU "bubble" we live in. The Food Bank is a judgment free space that provides a variety of nutritious food to anyone.

Visit Website
Loads of Love

This program was developed directly by JMU GIVE to improve the quality of life of those experiencing homelessness by providing a weekly laundry service to guests at Open Doors, ensuring regular access to fresh, clean clothes. Open Doors combats homelessness as a low barrier shelter for those who may not qualify for services elsewhere.
